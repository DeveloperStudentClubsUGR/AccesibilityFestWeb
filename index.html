About DyploFest

What is it?

DyploGest is an event based on Graphical User Interface (GUI). From frontend development to the use of non-visual interfaces such as voice assistants, whe want to bring new points of view to something that we all assume but only a few of us see on the everyday, working to let everyone use these useful technologies.

Talks and GUIs

Learn about a lot of topics in only one track with our talks and workshops about subjects related to this scene. Web, accessibility, design, Android... You only have to come and Learn with us.

Networking

This event want to connect professionals, companies and students that want to learn about the informatics market. Moreover, our speakers seek to satiate your thirst for knowledge and build alliances with companies to pursue new opportunities.

How do i get involved?

This event needs your collaboration to make it work, so if you want to support the cause, here are some ways to do it.

Come as a Speaker!

If you have something to show to the attenders, come and give a lecture! The main point of an event is to have content to show to the public, so any submission will be welcome.

What kind of talk?

  • GUI design and accessibility
  • Frontend technology and app development
  • Non graphic GUI: Chatbots, Sensors, actuators...

Or come as a volunteer

If you are from Malaga and you want to help us to make everything go well, you can fill this form to let us contact you.
